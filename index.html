Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the NESA LAB of Zhejiang University (ZJU) co-supervised by Prof. Xuhong Zhang, Prof. Wenhai Wang and Prof. Shouling Ji .

Before that, I received my Bachelor Degree in the school of Cyber Science and Engineering of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), where I had a great research experience supervised by Prof. Ming Wen in the SAS-HUST group. My research interests mainly focus on System Security, Open-Source Supply-chain Security and Program Analysis.

Currently I am working on Parallel Fuzzing and Security issues in NPM ecosystem. Feel free to contact me and ask anything.
